The Rare Earth Chokepoint: China Controls the Inputs to Every Technology You Invest In
The problem was identified in 2010. The policy response started in 2021. The market still hasn't fully priced it in.

There are 17 rare earth elements. They are not actually rare — the name is a misnomer. What is rare is the processing capacity to refine them into usable materials. That capacity is concentrated almost entirely in one country.

China controls approximately 85% of global rare earth processing. The United States imports 100% of its processed rare earth compounds. The DOD has named this a critical risk in every annual report since 2017. Now the policy money is moving — and where federal dollars go, contracts follow.

The trickle-down chain runs five steps: mining → separation → magnet manufacturing → motors and components → end products. Steps 1–3 are where Dragonfly is focused. That is where the supply constraint lives, where the policy money is directed, and where the stocks have the most room to move. MP Materials received a $58.5M DOD contract to build the first domestic magnet manufacturing facility in the United States. Questions

Plain answers.

Where do I find my referral link?
It's in every email from Dragonfly — look for the green banner near the top. It's your personal link, always active, always the same. No login required.
What does the person I refer get?
They subscribe at the standard price for whichever tier they choose. The referral rewards go to you. Nothing extra required from them — the link handles it automatically.
What counts as an "active" referral?
Someone you referred who currently has a paid, active subscription. If they cancel, they no longer count toward your referral total and your revenue share percentage adjusts. If they re-subscribe later, they count again.
Does time on Basic or Investor count toward the revenue share years requirement?
Yes — for the base grid, all years on any tier count. Only years specifically on Premium count toward the Premium continuous bonus. So upgrading to Premium later doesn't reset your clock — you just don't get the Premium bonus for years spent on lower tiers.
What happens if my subscription lapses?
Rewards pause while inactive. Your referral count, milestone progress, and year count all resume exactly where they left off when you re-subscribe. Nothing is lost — it just pauses.
When is revenue share paid?
Calculated at the end of each month, paid the following month. Based on net subscription revenue (after payment processing fees) from your active referred subscribers.
Is there a cap on referrals?
No cap on how many people you can refer. 12% is the maximum revenue share percentage, reached at Legend tier (270 refs) combined with 12+ years subscribed and Premium continuous status. There's no ceiling on how much that 12% can generate as your network grows.
Why do all the revenue share milestones add up to 9?
45, 63, 90, 108, 135, 162, 189, 243, 270 — every one of them. It's tied to the 3/6/9 principle that runs through how Dragonfly is built. It's not an accident.
