Dividend Income Planner → Open the app

Educational planning tool, not financial advice. It compounds the simple assumptions you enter (growth rates, contributions, debt rates) and pays your debts down month by month, rolling each freed payment into investing; it can also show everything in today's dollars (inflation). Real life varies, returns aren't guaranteed, and it still simplifies taxes — use it to build intuition for your trajectory, not as a precise forecast. Nothing you type is sent anywhere or stored — it lives only in this browser tab. Dragonfly Lens is not a registered investment advisor.
