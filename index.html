How it works

Eight signal sources. One alert when they align.

Most newsletters tell you what already happened. Dragonfly fires when something is actually happening — and routes you to the primary source document, not a summary of a summary.

📄

SEC EDGAR Form 4

Insider purchase filings — executives buying their own stock with personal money. Filed within 2 days. Only purchases counted, not option exercises.

🏛

Congressional Trades

House + Senate STOCK Act disclosures. Members with committee oversight of energy, defense, and critical minerals. Purchases and sales both tracked.

📈

CFTC Commitment of Traders

Weekly institutional positioning in gold, silver, copper, oil, and natural gas futures. Extreme net-short readings historically precede sharp reversals.

📄

Federal Register

DOE, NRC, and EPA rule changes affecting our sectors — often weeks before they reach financial media. Parsed daily for uranium, lithium, copper, and hydrogen keywords.

💡

USPTO Patents

Patent grants in graphene, SMR, solid-state batteries, optical computing, and quantum. R&D signals before commercial viability is priced in.

🏢

Government Grants

NRCan, DOE ARPA-E, Grants.gov, EU Horizon Europe, and SAM.gov contracts — pre-discovery capital flowing into sectors before the market notices.

📊

EIA Nuclear Data

US nuclear capacity utilization and outage data. Sustained outages increase near-term uranium demand as utilities burn stockpiles faster.

🌎

Macro Snapshot

DXY, 10Y Treasury, VIX, gold, copper, uranium ETF, and lithium ETF — updated live with every alert for regime context.
